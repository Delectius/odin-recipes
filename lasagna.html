Lasagna

Description

A recipe on how to make a delicious and flavorful lasagna quick, simple and easy. Enjoy it with your family and/or friends.

Ingredients

Filling:

  • 2 eggs
  • 1 cup of ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup of shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon of dried basil,oregano and parsley

Sauce:

  • 1 can dried tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 1 small can tomato paste + 1 small can water
  • 1 tablespoon sugar
  • 1 teaspoon of garlic powder and dried basil and oregano
  • 1 tablespoon of dried parsley

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 350 F degrees. Cook the lasagna noodle until al dente.
  2. In a large tray, cover it with a little bit of the sauce at the bottom. Layer the noodles then the sauce and then the filling.
  3. Repeate the previous step, until the lasagna tray is all filled up. Bake, uncovered, for 1 hour. Let stand 15 minutes before serving.
Enjoy!
