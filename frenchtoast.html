French Toast

Description

Who does not love french toast, a staple breakfast beloved by everybody.

Ingredients

  • 2 eggs
  • 30ml milk
  • cinnamon/sugar/nutmeg
  • 4 bread slices
  • 40g butter
  • Whipped cream and fruit toppings of your choice

Steps

  1. Mix the eggs, milk, sugar, cinnamon and the nutmeg in a bowl. Dip the bread in the mixture.
  2. Fry the dipped sliced in au sauce pan until golden brown. Serve with honey, fruit or maple syrup.
Enjoy!
