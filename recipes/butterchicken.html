Butter Chicken

Description

A popular and famous dish, that will make you crave for more after each bite. Served with cooked basmati rice, naan bread or even on its own, this dish will make you and all your loved ones happy.

Ingredients

For the marinade

  • 1 juiced lemon
  • 2 teaspoon ground cumin
  • 2 teaspoon paprika
  • 1-2 teaspoon hot chili powder
  • 200g natural yogurt

For the curry

  • 2 tablespoon vegetable oil
  • 1 large onion, chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, chopped
  • 300ml chicken stock
  • 1 teaspoon garam masala
  • chopped and deseeded green chilli and grated ginger
  • 3 tablespoon tomato puree
  • 2 teaspoon ground fenugreek

Steps

  1. In an appropriate sized bowl, mix all the the marinade ingredients and toss the chopped chicken with it. Make sure the chicken is well covered. Cover the bowl and chill it in the fridge overnight.
  2. In a large saucepan, heat the oil and then add the onion, garlic, green chili, ginger and some seasoning.Fry it for 10 mins.
  3. Add the spices tih the tomato puree, cook until fragrant then add the chicken stock and marinated chicken. Cook for 15 mins, add any marinade left from the bowl, simmer for 5 mins.
  4. Serve with rice, naan or bread.
Enjoy!
